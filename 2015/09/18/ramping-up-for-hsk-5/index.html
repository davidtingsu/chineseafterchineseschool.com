Discoveries

  • Google Drive OCR: My strong preference for reading online text is tightly coupled with the ability to use a popup dictionary such as ZhongWen to get immediate translations. Read this Google Drive OCR article by ChineseHacks
  • Memrise App Speed Review Mode for Android: Originally on the Memrise Android app, the review mode was not time pressured, but now I can race against the clock to see how well I know my words. Also, 2600 HSK5 vocabulary list that I need to keep fresh, this allows me to review more words in less time.
  • chinaSMACK: I arrived on this news site while browsing for swear words. Some of their articles such as Tibetan Mastiffs: From Guard Dogs to Status Symbols to Hot Pot have accompanying original content and original comments in addition to the translations. The site is now following a digest format and not producing lengthy content, which resulted in an uproar on reddit.
  • HanPing Chinese Popup for Android: Finally, a popup tool for mobile phones. Alas, the app is not compatible with my Samsung Galaxy S4, but I’ll be sure to get it on my next phone upgrade.
  • ZhiHu (知乎): Found this link on reddit. Mandarin version of Quora
